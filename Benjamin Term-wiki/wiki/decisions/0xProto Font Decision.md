# 0xProto Font Decision

## Decision

Use 0xProto as the bundled BenjaminTerm identity font.

## Rationale

0xProto is hackerish, readable, and legally redistributable under the SIL Open Font License 1.1.

## Consequences

BenjaminTerm avoids depending on users installing a local font and avoids redistributing OCR A Extended without a known redistribution license.
