# Hyper Yap

Hyper Yap is BenjaminTerm's sister project. It owns speech-to-text, smart paste, copy/paste helpers, and image-paste workflows.

Implication for BenjaminTerm: do not rebuild paste automation inside the terminal unless a future workflow clearly cannot live in Hyper Yap.
